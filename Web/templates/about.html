Data Science for Education

Empowering Teachers through Predictive Analytics

Objective

Every day, educators must face the immense challenge of identifying students that need the most help. If students that are at risk of falling behind don't get sufficient help, they may fall even further behind and eventually drop out. Our goal is to bridge the gap between teachers and their students by clearly indicating which students may need assistance.

Method

We utilized this dataset. It contains data detailing various aspects of 4,400 students' lives (such as age, GPA, etc.) as well as whether they dropped out of school or not. We used a sequential model with two dense layers to analyze the data.
