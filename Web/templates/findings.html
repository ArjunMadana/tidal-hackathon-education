Interesting Insights

Ranked in order of highest effect
- Past GPA was the most important factor in student retention
- Being up to date on tuition payments was the second most important factor (Being behind indicated a higher drop out rate)
- Having financial aid increased student retention
- Having debt decreased student retention
- Males are more likely to drop out
